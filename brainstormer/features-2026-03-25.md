# Feature Suggestions — 2026-03-25

## WHY PEOPLE WANT THIS

The core itch: everyone knows they're being lied to, nobody knows what to do about it. Triangulate resolves the paradox — you don't have to trust anyone if you can see where enemies agree.

Three demand drivers:
1. **Trust collapse** — media consolidation, AI content, contested government data
2. **Polarization fatigue** — "just tell me what's true" is massive and underserved
3. **AI anxiety meets curiosity** — "we don't trust one AI either" is disarming

The share trigger is built in: "Fox News and MSNBC agreed on something" is inherently surprising.

## KEY INSIGHT

The bottleneck is DISTRIBUTION, not analysis. The product is compelling enough that exposure converts. Engineer as many paths to the "wait, they BOTH said that?" moment as possible. Convergence Card, Morning Brief, Journalist Badge, Browser Extension — these are all distribution mechanisms.

Phase 5A creates the Journalist Pro justification. The Data Base makes $14.99/month feel obvious because it's a growing personal asset, not a feature rental.
