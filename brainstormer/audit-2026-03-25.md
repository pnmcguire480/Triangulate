### Convergence Algorithm

```
1. Get unique bias tiers of supporting articles
2. Calculate ideological spread (max distance / 6)
3. Bonuses: +0.15 cross-center, +0.1 CENTER anchor, +0.1 multi-region
4. Count factor: 2=0.7x, 3=0.85x, 4+=1.0x
5. Fringe guard: cap at 0.2 if all FAR tiers
6. Clamp [0, 1], round 2 decimals
```
