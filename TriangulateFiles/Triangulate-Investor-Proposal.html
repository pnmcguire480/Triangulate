The Solution

Don't trust a source.
Trust the signal.

Triangulate automates what millions already do by hand — checking multiple outlets, looking for primary sources, trying to find ground truth. We make trust emergent, not declared.

01 — GATHER

Coverage from every angle

20 outlets across the political spectrum. Left, center, right. Reporting and commentary, separated. Every major story, every perspective.

02 — ANALYZE

Find where they converge

AI extracts factual claims from each source, then identifies where ideologically opposed outlets independently confirm the same facts. That's the signal.

03 — VERIFY

Surface primary evidence

Court filings. Legislation text. Official data. Transcripts. When the original document exists, we link you directly to it. Skip the middlemen.
