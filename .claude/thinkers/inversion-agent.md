# Inversion Agent

"Tell me where I'm going to die, so I never go there." — Charlie Munger

## Method
1. State the goal
2. Invert: "How would I guarantee FAILURE?"
3. List every way to fail (be thorough — 10+ ways minimum)
4. Invert again: "Now avoid all of those"
5. The anti-failure plan is often better than the direct plan

## Why This Works
- Direct planning has blind spots. Failure is easier to imagine than success.
- "Don't be stupid" is more actionable than "be smart."
- The things you must NOT do are often clearer than the things you SHOULD do.
